DISCOVER SPAIN

CULTURE

Spain is known for its vibrant and diverse culture, influenced by its rich history and regional traditions.

The Spanish language, art, music, and dance play a significant role in the country's cultural identity. Flamenco, a passionate and expressive form of dance and music, is particularly emblematic of Spanish culture.

Spain is also famous for its cuisine, with dishes like paella, tapas, and sangria capturing the essence of Spanish gastronomy.

TOP TOURIST DESTINATIONS

When visiting Spain, there are several must-see tourist destinations:

  • Barcelona: Explore the vibrant city of Barcelona, known for its unique architecture, including the famous Sagrada Familia and Park Güell.
  • Madrid: Discover the capital city of Spain, home to world-class museums like the Prado Museum and iconic landmarks such as the Royal Palace.
  • Seville: Immerse yourself in the rich history and culture of Seville, with its stunning Alcázar Palace, Gothic Cathedral, and lively flamenco scene.
  • Granada: Visit the enchanting city of Granada and marvel at the architectural masterpiece of the Alhambra, a stunning Moorish palace.
  • Valencia: Enjoy the beautiful beaches of Valencia and visit the futuristic City of Arts and Sciences, a remarkable architectural complex.
  • Andalusia: Explore the picturesque region of Andalusia, famous for its white-washed villages, such as Ronda and Mijas, and the vibrant city of Cordoba with its stunning Mosque-Cathedral.
