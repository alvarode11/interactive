EXPLORE JAPAN

CULTURE

Japan is a country with a unique and fascinating culture deeply rooted in tradition.

The Japanese people are known for their politeness, discipline, and attention to detail.

Japanese cuisine is celebrated worldwide, with popular dishes such as sushi, ramen, and tempura.

TOP TOURIST DESTINATIONS

When visiting Japan, there are several must-see tourist destinations:

  • Tokyo: Explore the bustling metropolis of Tokyo, with its futuristic skyscrapers, historic temples, and vibrant neighborhoods.
  • Kyoto: Visit Kyoto, the cultural heart of Japan, known for its traditional wooden houses, stunning gardens, and iconic temples like Kinkaku-ji (Golden Pavilion).
  • Hiroshima Peace Memorial Park: Reflect on the history and visit the memorial park dedicated to the victims of the atomic bombing in 1945.
  • Mount Fuji: Experience the beauty of Mount Fuji, Japan's tallest peak and a UNESCO World Heritage site. You can climb the mountain or admire it from various viewpoints.
  • Nara: Encounter friendly deer and explore the ancient temples and shrines of Nara, including Todai-ji Temple with its Great Buddha.
  • Hakone: Relax in the hot springs of Hakone and enjoy picturesque views of Mount Fuji while cruising on Lake Ashi.
