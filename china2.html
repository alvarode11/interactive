DISCOVER CHINA

CULTURE

China is a country with a rich and diverse culture that spans thousands of years.

The Chinese civilization has made significant contributions to art, literature, philosophy, science, and technology.

Chinese cuisine, with its wide variety of flavors and regional dishes, is famous worldwide. Popular dishes include Peking duck, dim sum, and Sichuan cuisine.

TOP TOURIST DESTINATIONS

When visiting China, there are several must-see tourist destinations:

  • Great Wall of China: Explore one of the world's most iconic landmarks, offering breathtaking views and a glimpse into China's ancient history.
  • Forbidden City: Visit the imperial palace complex in Beijing, which served as the home of Chinese emperors for nearly 500 years.
  • Terracotta Army: Marvel at the life-sized terracotta sculptures of soldiers, horses, and chariots that were buried with China's first emperor, Qin Shi Huang.
  • Li River: Cruise along the picturesque Li River in Guilin, surrounded by stunning limestone karst landscapes.
  • Shanghai: Experience the vibrant cosmopolitan city known for its modern skyline, historic Bund waterfront, and bustling markets.
  • Yellow Mountain: Hike the scenic trails of Huangshan, known as Yellow Mountain, and be immersed in its stunning natural beauty.
